# Shock Sensitivity Index: Demographic Vulnerability in the 2008 & COVID-19 Downturns

An interactive Power BI dashboard examining which demographic groups are hit hardest — and recover slowest — after major economic shocks.

![Dashboard Screenshot](assets/dashboard-screenshot.png)
## Overview

Unemployment doesn't hit every group equally, and it doesn't recover evenly either. This dashboard compares the 2008 Financial Crisis and COVID-19 across sex, age, and race/ethnicity to identify which groups were most severely impacted by each shock, and which are still recovering years later.

## Target Audience

Policymakers, workforce development agencies, and organizational leaders who design employment programs and allocate resources during economic downturns — including organizations like the Department of Labor focused on equitable access to unemployment insurance.

## Research Questions

- **RQ1:** Which demographic groups were most severely impacted by the 2008 financial crisis and COVID-19, and was one shock worse than the other for specific groups?
- **RQ2:** Which demographic groups recover slowest after economic shocks, and where should workforce interventions be prioritized?

## Key Findings

- Unemployment gaps exist across sex, age, and race/ethnicity even during stable economic periods 
- Both crises widened these gaps sharply — youth unemployment (ages 16–19) peaked at **22.9%**, and unemployment among African Americans reached **14.1%** (**Conflict**)
- Recovery has been slow and uneven: youth unemployment remained at **14%** as of 2025, years after the initial shock (**Resolution**)

## How the Dashboard Guides Decision-Making

1. **Shock Sensitivity Index table** — identifies which groups are most vulnerable *before* a crisis occurs, enabling proactive resource allocation
2. **Conflict bar chart** — directly compares Financial Crisis and COVID-19 impacts across all demographic groups, helping leaders weight emergency relief funding
3. **Post-COVID recovery trajectory chart** — monitors which groups lag behind years after a crisis, preventing premature withdrawal of workforce support programs

## How to Use the Dashboard

Use the **Group slicer** to filter all visuals by demographic category. Click a tile to select or remove groups. Use **Select All** to reset your choices.

## Tech Stack

- **Power BI** — dashboard design and interactivity
- **DAX** — calculated measures, including the Shock Sensitivity Index
- **Power Query** — data cleaning and transformation
